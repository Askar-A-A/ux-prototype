Status & Points

Your “status” on OpenUnited

Primary OpenUnited activities relate to Challenges and their Bounties, Ideas & Bugs. Whilst earning payments by completing payment-eligible Bounties requires that you have first built your status to "Honeybee" or higher, you can claim Bounties on Challenges immediately after joining.

Irrespective of status, all users are able to submit Ideas & Bugs on public products and to claim most Bounties.

So, if you haven’t already; please join OpenUnited and get started today.

What is meant by “status”?

Your status on OpenUnited reflects your level of engagement and the trust you have earnt using the platform. Your status is measured in points, and you earn and lose points by performing actions on OpenUnited

Your status increases when:

  • You successfully complete Bounties on Challenges (points depend on the Bounty)
  • Ideas & Bugs you create are accepted by the related product team
  • Your accepted Ideas & Bugs are upvoted
  • As a Manager or Admin of a product, Bounties that you create are claimed and completed
  • You invite a person and they successfully register on OpenUnited
  • The user you invited completes their first bounty successfully

Your status decreases when:

  • You claim bounties and do not complete them successfully
  • You receive a warning for violating terms of use

Some OpenUnited functionality, such as the ability to earn payment for payment-eligible Bounties, requires a particular status. Ultimately, the more you use openunited.com in a constructive way, the more points you earn, the higher your status and the more privileges you unlock.
